## Projects

<div class="project">
  <h3>GenomicFormer</h3>
  <p>A transformer architecture optimized for very long genomic sequences. We introduce a sparse local-global attention scheme that reduces memory complexity from O(n²) to O(n log n) while maintaining strong performance on downstream tasks such as regulatory element prediction and variant effect scoring.</p>
  <p>
    <a href="#">Paper</a> &nbsp;·&nbsp;
    <a href="https://github.com/savannahferretti">Code</a>
  </p>
</div>

<div class="project">
  <h3>DrugGraphNet</h3>
  <p>A graph neural network framework for predicting drug–target interactions at scale. DrugGraphNet encodes molecular graphs and protein contact maps jointly, enabling zero-shot generalization to novel compounds. Trained on a curated dataset of 2M+ compound–protein pairs.</p>
  <p>
    <a href="#">Paper</a> &nbsp;·&nbsp;
    <a href="https://github.com/savannahferretti">Code</a> &nbsp;·&nbsp;
    <a href="#">Demo</a>
  </p>
</div>

<div class="project">
  <h3>scIntegrate</h3>
  <p>A contrastive learning framework for integrating single-cell RNA sequencing datasets across experimental batches, technologies, and organisms. scIntegrate achieves state-of-the-art alignment scores on the Open Problems in Single-Cell Analysis benchmarks.</p>
  <p>
    <a href="#">Paper</a> &nbsp;·&nbsp;
    <a href="https://github.com/savannahferretti">Code</a>
  </p>
</div>

<div class="project">
  <h3>SparseVAE-Bio</h3>
  <p>A sparse variational autoencoder with biologically-informed priors for learning interpretable latent representations of gene expression data. Latent dimensions align with known biological pathways, enabling mechanistic insight alongside predictive accuracy.</p>
